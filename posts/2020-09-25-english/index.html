Writing: 24th September, 2020 - Airbus unveils plans for zero-emission planes.

Summary

The Airbus predicts that hydrogen propulsion fits for fuel of the future airplanes. However, they need to prepare the refuelling infrastructure. Anyway, they think that the fuel can change the aviation industry.
