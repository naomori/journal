# Study supplement

## Part.2

## Vocabulary

### inventory, SYN: stock
1. a list of all the things in a place
2. all the goods in a shop

### drag
1. to pull something along the ground, often because it is too heavy to carry

### paw
1. an animal's foot that has nails or claws
